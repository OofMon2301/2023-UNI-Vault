# [[Projection Formula]]

# Definition of Projection Formula:
*Created on 16:37 10-07-2023

$$
\mathbf{w}_{1}=\frac{((\mathbf{w})\cdot \mathbf{v})}{||\mathbf{v}||^{2}}\mathbf{v}\quad , \quad \mathbf{w}_{2}=\mathbf{w}-\frac{((\mathbf{w})\cdot \mathbf{v})}{||\mathbf{v}||^{2}}\mathbf{v}
$$
